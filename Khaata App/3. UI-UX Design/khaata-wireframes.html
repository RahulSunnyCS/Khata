Section 01 / 09

Onboarding & Auth

First five minutes determine retention. We optimize for fastest time to first expense: phone OTP only, profile fields are minimal and skippable, and a guest mode path lets users start tracking before signing up. KYC is deferred until they actually try to settle via UPI Collect (FR-USR-05). All copy is short — six languages must fit (FR-USR-06).

Section 06 / 09

Settlement — The Moat

This is the wedge against Splitwise. Three rails: UPI deep-link (V1 — opens GPay/PhonePe with prefilled VPA + amount), UPI Collect (V1.5 — payee initiates a request via Razorpay, webhook auto-marks settled), and cross-group netting (V1 — single tap to net opposing balances across groups). Manual cash settlement always available. Every successful settlement writes ledger_entries in the opposite direction — same idea as your le010 reversing rent debt.
